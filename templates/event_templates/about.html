Events is used to find any events near you or at a certain Location.

Browse any Event

You can browse events by searching through the search bar in the header and footer. You can also see the most popular events.

Event Participation

You can opt in and out of events to allow others to see how many participants an event will have, and get an accurate count.

Create an Event

Create your own event in the profile page. View all the events you created as well as how many participants are in each. You are also allowed to delete your events

Find specific Events

You can search for events, view and share the events, and find events based on category, date of creation, or date of event.

Unique Event links

Share your event through the uniquely generated link for each event. Just click on the event title.
